Two Truths & a Lie

In this project, you will state two facts about yourself and one lie, and we have to guess which one is which!

Brainstorming

For this project we need to come up with two truth statements, and one lie.

Start thinking about two facts about yourself. Do you have brown hair? Do you have two dogs? Or ten!

Don't forget to think about your lie. This can be something fun, silly, or un-believable! The more extreme and out there your truths and lies are, the more fun this game is to play with your friends!
