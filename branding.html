What is a brand?

From freelancers, to small companies and large ones, all have a brand. Designers are all about design continuity. This helps a brand looking clean, so you don’t confuse them with another company. Designers will pick a specific logo, as well as brand colours that help you associate with what they have to offer.

Watch this video about Branding Identity

Taking what you have learned about branding, now time to apply.

Your older cousin has just started his very own coffee shop! He found out you are learning GraphicDesign and wants to be your first customer. He has asked you to design him a brand identity, which includes his very own color palette, as well as custom logo, so that when he has his website built, he will have brand continuity. He wants clean designs, warm tones. For his logo, he wants his coffee to attract customers interested in saving the environment, or helping animals, not just a hot cup of java. He has given you an example of what kind of logo he is looking for, and can’t wait to hear what his favorite cousin has planned.
