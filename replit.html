Lesson 0 - Creating a repl.it

Background Info

Repl.it is a powerful online IDE, compiler and interpreter that can be used to code in multiple programming languages.

End Product

Repl.it account


Let's get started!

Navigate to repl.it

Click on Sign Up

Create a new account

You can either create a new account, or link your Google, Github or Facebook account.

To link an account, just click on that icon. You may be prompted to sign into the account and give repl.it permissions. Read them carefully, then click agree if you agree.

To create a new account, simply choose a username and password. Enter these as well as your email and click sign up! Feel free to read the terms of service and privacy policy, as you are agreeing to these when you sign up.

Now you have a repl.it account!
This is where you will create and store your Python scripts.
